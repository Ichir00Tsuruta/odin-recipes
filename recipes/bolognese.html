Pasta Bolognese

Do you miss your nonna's pasta bolognese?

My nonna used to make me pasta bolognese when I was sad. Nothing better than the umami to cheer you up!

This recipe will give you 4 servings!

Ingredients

  • Rigatoni or any pasta of your choice. Plz do not use angel hair for the love of the god
  • 1 stalk of celery
  • 1 small carrot
  • 1 onion
  • ~3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • Salt
  • ~3oz diced pancetta
  • 1 cup dry white wine
  • 1/3 cup tomato paste
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Grated nutmeg
  • 2 cup chicken stock
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • Grated parmesan

Steps

  1. Nonna just did it. So you should too
  2. Just simmer and sprinkle some love!
